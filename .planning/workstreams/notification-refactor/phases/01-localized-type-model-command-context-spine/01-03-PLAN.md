<objective>
Migrate the seven marketplace-family commands (add, remove, list, info, update,
autoupdate, noautoupdate) plus the bootstrap delegator to the command-local
notification model: each gets a co-located `<cmd>.messaging.ts` declaring its
`CommandContext` (with `Messaging.label`), private mp-status tuple, render map total
over its own mp-statuses (lifting the `renderMpHeader` switch arms verbatim), and
command-private reasons. Orchestrators thread `notifyWithContext` at their call sites
with Single/Plural cardinality annotation.

marketplace status sets into the owning vertical slices (MOD-01/03), localizing the
missing-arm-is-a-compile-error guarantee (D-10), output-neutral.

Output: 6 new `.messaging.ts` sibling modules (autoupdate hosts both autoupdate and
noautoupdate contexts since they share one orchestrator) + orchestrator call-site
rewires + bootstrap delegation wiring. Implements MOD-01/MOD-02/MOD-03 + OUT-07 for
the marketplace family. catalog-uat + notify-v2 stay green.
</objective>
