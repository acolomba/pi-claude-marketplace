<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### Architecture: widen the typed outcome contract (not parse `postCommitWarnings`)

- **D-01 [LOCKED]** Widen `InstallPluginOutcome`'s `"installed"` discriminated-union variant (defined at `extensions/pi-claude-marketplace/orchestrators/plugin/install.ts:113-122`) to carry `readonly declaresAgents: boolean` and `readonly declaresMcp: boolean`. The fields are **required**, not optional -- the install-time predicate is always knowable from `installCtx` and a discriminated outcome must not have a third undefined state (NFR-7).

- **D-02 [LOCKED]** Compute the predicates inside `install.ts` from the same staged-name accessors already used for the standalone cascade row (`install.ts:751-752`): `declaresAgents: installCtx.stagedAgentNames.length > 0` and `declaresMcp: installCtx.stagedMcpServerNames.length > 0`. Do **not** introduce a second derivation path -- single-source the predicate.

- **D-03 [LOCKED]** Widen `PluginInstalledOutcome` in `extensions/pi-claude-marketplace/orchestrators/import/execute.ts:49-57` to carry `readonly declaresAgents: boolean` and `readonly declaresMcp: boolean` (required, matching D-01). The import-internal outcome type mirrors the upstream `InstallPluginOutcome` surface so the propagation is a typed plumbing, not a re-derivation.

- **D-04 [LOCKED]** At `import/execute.ts:474-475`, replace the hard-coded literals with `declaresAgents: o.declaresAgents` and `declaresMcp: o.declaresMcp`. Delete the deferred-work comment at lines 467-473; the cascade row now consumes the typed predicates directly.

- **D-05 [LOCKED]** The case-`"installed"` branch at `import/execute.ts:835-861` already extracts `outcome.resourcesChanged`; extend it to extract `outcome.declaresAgents` and `outcome.declaresMcp` and push them onto the `PluginInstalledOutcome` record at lines 836-844.

- **D-06 [LOCKED]** Do **not** infer the predicates from `outcome.postCommitWarnings` string parsing. The source comment at `execute.ts:469-473` flags two options -- postCommitWarnings parsing OR widening `PluginInstalledOutcome` -- and explicitly favors the latter; postCommitWarnings is a free-text warning vector, not a typed predicate surface.

- **Outcome propagation in import/execute.ts:835-844** -- current:
  ```ts
  case "installed":
    result.installedPlugins.push({
      kind: "plugin-installed",
      scope: plugin.scope,

  ```

  ```ts
  case "installed":
    result.installedPlugins.push({
      kind: "plugin-installed",
  ```
