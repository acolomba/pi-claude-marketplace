<objective>
Thread the derived force signal into the `info` detail surface and the
install/update success notifications (D-66-04, FSTAT-07). This resolves Open
Question Q2: the success-notification force arm is selected by
`resolved.state === "unsupported"`, building a `PluginForceInstalledMessage`
(the TransitionMessageBase arm added in 66-01) instead of the plain installed/
updated arm.

`info` currently mislabels an installed-but-unsupported plugin as `installed`;
this plan flips that single branch to `force-installed`, inheriting the
dropped-component brace already threaded through `narrowUnsupportedKinds`.

Purpose: make `info` truthful about force-installed plugins and make forced
install/update success rows read "force-installed".
Output: discriminated branches in info.ts/install.ts/update.ts plus info unit
coverage.
</objective>
