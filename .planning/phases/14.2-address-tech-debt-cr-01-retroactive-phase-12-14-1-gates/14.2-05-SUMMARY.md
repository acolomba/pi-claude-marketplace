## Investigation Note: ROADMAP.md Coverage auto-flip gap

This 14.2-05 work is remediation, not a structural fix. The 29 Phase-13 +
1 Phase-14.1 ROADMAP.md staleness surface exists because:

1. `/gsd:execute-phase` does NOT currently auto-flip ROADMAP.md
   `## Coverage (v1.3)` checkboxes when a plan completes. The CMC-NN row
   remains `Pending` after the plan that addresses it lands, even though
   `REQUIREMENTS.md ## Coverage` (the authoritative source) was updated by
   the workflow.

2. The 14.2-05 plan flips the 29 Phase-13 rows by hand to match
   REQUIREMENTS.md. This closes the immediate drift but does NOT prevent
   recurrence.

3. The structural fix (teaching `/gsd:execute-phase` to auto-flip both
   Coverage tables on plan completion) belongs in the GSD plugin (separate
   project from `pi-claude-marketplace`). It is explicitly out of scope per
   D-14.

4. No project-local architecture test for ROADMAP <-> REQUIREMENTS parity is
   added; this option was rejected in CONTEXT.md `<deferred>` because this
   project should not couple to GSD doc structure.

Additional observation (not fixed in this plan): the 6 Phase-12 CMC rows
(CMC-08, CMC-11, CMC-14, CMC-19, CMC-36, CMC-37) in `## Coverage (v1.3)`
also still read `Pending` despite REQUIREMENTS.md marking them `Complete`.
D-13 explicitly scoped this plan to the 29 Phase-13 entries; the Phase-12
drift is noted here for traceability but not fixed.
