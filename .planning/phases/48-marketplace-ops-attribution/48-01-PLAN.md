<objective>
Land the D-48-A type-model foundation (the linchpin) and ATTR-07 (`marketplace add` precondition
attribution) atomically in one GREEN commit. This is the FIRST plan in the serialized
marketplace-op wave because `marketplace add` is the only op that genuinely NEEDS the new
`MpFailed.reasons?` field -- the type+renderer+proof change MUST land with the first op that uses it,
with NO intermediate RED.

`marketplace add` currently throws raw on every precondition failure (unknown/unsupported source,
duplicate name, stale clone, path-source missing, invalid manifest), and the five matching `REASONS`
members (`duplicate name`, `stale clone`, `unsupported source`, `source missing`, `invalid manifest`)
are defined-but-never-routed-through-`notify` (audit M-7). After this plan, each renders a structured
`⊘ <name> [<scope>] (failed) {<reason>}` row on the marketplace subject.

Also introduce the typed `InvalidMarketplaceManifestError` (D-48-B Option B2) in `shared/errors.ts`,
thrown by `domain/manifest.ts::loadMarketplaceManifestUncached`. It is consumed by ATTR-07 here AND
by ATTR-10 (Plan 48-03), giving both paths a structural (not substring) manifest-failure classifier.

Purpose: Make the marketplace subject capable of rendering its own closed-set reason (D-48-A), and
make `add`'s five defined-but-unrouted reasons reachable end-to-end (ATTR-07).
Output: Extended `MpFailed` arm + renderer + inverted type proof; typed manifest error; notify-routed
`add` precondition failures; 5 new catalog states + fixtures; updated `add` tests.
</objective>
